About

Capturing the essence of the deep.

Bubbly Depths is a seltzer brand designed to capture the essence of the underwater realm and provide a revitalizing experience for your taste buds. The visual identity draws from the ocean's color palette, light behavior, and organic forms, translating the feeling of diving into a can you can hold in your hand.

The design challenge was to create packaging that feels premium and refreshing simultaneously, standing out on a crowded shelf while communicating the brand's oceanic inspiration at a glance.

The refreshing world of the ocean, captured in a can.
Product design

From label to lifestyle.

The visual system extends beyond the can itself; into product photography, social assets, and point-of-sale materials. Each touchpoint reinforces the underwater world through color gradients that shift from deep ocean blues to surface-level teals, organic bubble textures, and typography that feels fluid without losing legibility.
